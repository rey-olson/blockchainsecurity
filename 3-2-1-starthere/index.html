What

Welcome to this blog documenting my learning journey into the "cryptoverse", or the rabbit hole that is blockchain security.

I will focus primarily on:

  • Blockchain security in general (blockchain or protocol agnostic)
  • Smart contract security & audits
  • Risks related to decentralized finance (DeFi)
  • Ethereum security & Ethereum virtual machine (EVM)
  • Etc.

Please make sure to use the tags to filter through the various subjects.

The more you know, the more you realize you don't know.

Why

There is currently an extreme shortage of knowledgeable security professionals in the job market, and I reckon the shortage in the field of blockchain security is even more pressing.

Since sharing is caring, I hope to inspire others with my (albeit nascent) knowledge, and information I have come across through my searches on the internet.¬†
