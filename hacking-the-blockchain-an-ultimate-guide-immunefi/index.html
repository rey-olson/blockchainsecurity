Hacking the Blockchain: An Ultimate Guide

0xSleepy published a Medium article on his journey in becoming a blockchain security researcher:

https://medium.com/immunefi/hacking-the-blockchain-an-ultimate-guide-4f34b33c6e8b 

TLDR* on how to break into the blockchain security space;

  1. freeCodeCamp course (on Blockchain, Solidity & smart contracts)
  2. Chapters 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 13 and 14 in Mastering Ethereum (Optional)
  3. CryptoZombies
  4. Hack Solidity
  5. Smart Contract Security Chapter in Mastering Ethereum (Optional)
  6. SWC registry
  7. Secureum bootcamp
  8. CTFs (Ethernaut/CaptureTheEther -> Damn Vulnerable DeFi -> Paradigm CTF) 
  9. Real life experience/job (☞ ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)☞

(*) I slightly edited his summary: lay-out and links
